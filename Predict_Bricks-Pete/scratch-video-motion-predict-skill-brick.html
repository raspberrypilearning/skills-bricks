Scratch Video Motion

Predict Webcam

What does it do?

Scratch Video Motion lets a Scratch project watch movement through the webcam.

It does not know what the moving thing is. It gives a motion score your project can treat as a prediction.

Your projects can use motion to react to waving, jumping, speedy hands, or a very dramatic entrance.
