Scratch Body Pose

Predict Gesture

What does it do?

Scratch Body Pose uses the webcam to look for a body shape.

The model predicts a gesture, such as arm raised, hands apart, or standing still.

Your projects can use body poses as invisible buttons for games, art, and silly stage controls.
