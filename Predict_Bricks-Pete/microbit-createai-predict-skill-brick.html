Predict Movement

What does it do?

CreateAI uses micro:bit accelerometer examples to train movement labels.

It predicts the movement type, such as shake, tilt, spin, or still, with a confidence score.

Your projects can use the prediction to light LEDs, play sound, send radio, or control a pin.
