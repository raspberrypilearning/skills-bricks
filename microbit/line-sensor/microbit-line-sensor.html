Line Sensor

Detect Surface

What does it do?

A line sensor shines infrared light down and measures how much bounces back.

A dark line reflects little light and a pale surface reflects a lot, so the sensor can tell them apart.

With a micro:bit, you read the sensor on a pin to follow lines or spot edges.

Most modules output digital (1 and 0 for line and no line). Some have an analog output — read it with read_analog() instead. Adjust the module's sensitivity screw.
