micro:bit Temperature

Detect Level

What does it do?

The micro:bit can measure temperature in degrees Celsius using a sensor on its main chip.

It reads the temperature of the board, which is usually close to the room temperature.

Your projects can react to warm or cold, log readings, or warn about heat.

MicroPython

MakeCode

basic.forever(function () {
    let temp = input.temperature()
    basic.showNumber(temp)
})
temperature() returns the chip temperature in °C. It can read a little high because it senses the board, not the air.
