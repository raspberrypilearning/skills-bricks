micro:bit UART

Connect Serial

What does it do?

UART is a simple serial link that sends messages one bit after another.

Your micro:bit can use a TX pin to send and an RX pin to listen.

Use UART with GPS receivers, other boards, serial modules, or computer messages.

JavaScript

serial.redirect(
    SerialPin.P0,
    SerialPin.P1,
    BaudRate.BaudRate9600

For many serial modules, connect the module's TX to the micro:bit's RX, and the module's RX to the micro:bit's TX. Share GND too.
